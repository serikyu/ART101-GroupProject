Welcome To Hot Slugs Near You!

Introduction

Welcome to Hot Slugs Near You! A silly name, we know LOL. But we hope that this website can act as a fun way for UCSC Students, and Santa Cruz residents alike, to share their banna slugs! It's quite simple really. Find a slug? Take a pic and post it to our site! Add a fun caption and make sure to include where you spotted your slug. Your post will appear on our site's map as a marker that other users can click on, view, and comment!

Tutorial

Step 1:

Press start on the homepage to begin! You probably already figured this part out. From there, take a look at our map! Click and drag on the map to move around and view other areas.

Step 2:

Go ahead and click on a marker! Find a slug marker on the map and click it. Ta-da! Another user's post will pop-up. From there, you can see the user's profile name, the image of the slug they found, where it was found, and other details/information. You can even comment on the user's post! Click the "X" in the top right to exit.

Step 3:

Now it's time to make your own post! Scroll past the map on the main page and find the "I Found A Slug" button. Clicking here will prompt a pop-up for you to create your own post! Exit any time through "X" and minimize it for later using the "__".

Step 4:

Be sure to include the image of your slug, where it was found, and any other interesting details you think are post worthy (Ex: Was wet, on a tree, under a rock, etc.)! Click Submit and you're done! Feel free to revisit your post anytime and respond to people's comments! [Note: Posts are automatically removed from the site after 3 months.]
